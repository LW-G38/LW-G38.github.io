Intelligent Workplace

The Intelligent Workplace is a 'living' and 'lived in' lab. It is located at the top of Margaret Morrison Carnegie Hall. Operated in 1997, the researchers living in the lab conduct research projects on all the aspects of building performance, making the lab updated to the new technologies and energy- efficient.

The IW is 7500 sq ft. It includes a conference space, several open and enclosed offices, a kitchen and a classroom. The lighting system and the envelope system make it possible to take advantage of daylight. And the efficient HVAC system makes space more energy efficient.

It has won awards like AIA Honor Award for Architecture and Design and Business Week /Architectural Record Award. Faculty, researchers and students teach, work and study in the lab. And students in the MSSD, MSBPD, and PhD-BPD programs call it their home.
